Support & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I add a new item?

A: Go to the Inventory page, fill in all required fields, and click "Add Item".

Q: How do I update an item?

A: Enter the item name, new quantity/price, and click "Update".

Q: How do I delete an item?

A: Enter the item name and click "Delete".

Q: Where is my data stored?

A: Data is stored locally in your browser's session storage.
