Support & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I add a new item?
A: You can go to the Inventory page, fill out the forms, then click the Add Item button.

Q: How do I update an item?
A: Type in the name of the item you want to change, enter the new values, and press Update.

Q: How do I delete an item?
A: Just enter the item name and click the Delete button to remove it.

Q: Where is my data stored?
A: Your data is saved locally in your browser, not on any server.

Contact Support

If you have any issues while using the system, you can contact the administrator for help.
